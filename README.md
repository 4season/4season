# Hello, I'm Junho Heo

I am an undergraduate researcher in Computer Science interested in computational neuroscience, human learning and decision-making, and computational psychiatry.

My experiences with variations in attention, arousal, and learning efficiency led me to ask why people process the same information differently and make different decisions. I aim to study these differences using computational models, behavioral data, and neural data.

## Research Interests

- Computational neuroscience
- Reinforcement learning and decision-making
- Behavioral and neural data analysis
- Computational psychiatry

## Current Focus

I have experience in data preprocessing, machine learning model implementation, and backend development through projects involving MLPs, CNNs, structured and unstructured data, and API systems.

I am currently expanding this foundation toward reinforcement learning, probabilistic modeling, and computational analysis of behavioral and neural data.

## Selected Projects

- **ConvDDI** — CNN-based drug–drug interaction prediction
- **TrendXiv** — Analysis and prediction of arXiv research trends
- **Bus Delay Prediction** — MLP-based prediction of bus arrival delays
- **Magnolia** — Backend and notification-processing system
